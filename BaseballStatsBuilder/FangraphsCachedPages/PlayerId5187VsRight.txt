Freese also benefited from 16 infield hits despite well-below-average speed. His strikeout rate spiked to 28.9%. Overall, the Pirates liked what they saw enough to extend him two years with an option. I see a 33-year-old on the verge of collapse. Free agent wins cost over $8 million apiece, and Freese is being paid like a backup. Considering he's produced two wins in each of the last three seasons, there's a good chance he'll outperform his contract. Unfortunately, he's limited to first and third base, and both are positions of depth for the Pirates -- and in fantasy.
