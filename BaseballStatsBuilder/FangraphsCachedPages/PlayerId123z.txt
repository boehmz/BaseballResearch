You're probably right to, as he fizzled late in the season, came back as a reliever, and is now firmly outside of the top five or six spots in the rotation. With a below-average fastball and a starter's arsenal, you might think that Whitney's plus command would even things out. It might, if he were in the National League in a nice park.
