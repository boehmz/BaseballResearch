While he gave up fewer line drives in ‘09 (14.9%) than in ‘08 (21.8%), they turned into fly balls (35.4% in ‘08, 40.9% in ‘09), which, as we've seen, went out of the park with greater frequency. Even with a less-than-awesome infield defense in Chicago, ground balls are pretty much always a good idea, since they can't go out of the park. The White Sox are a middling team in 2010, so don't expect more than 12-13 wins for Danks (if you chase wins), but 190 innings, a 4.20 ERA, 150 strikeouts, and a 1.35 WHIP are safe bets.
